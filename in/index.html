Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up to work with The Medical Commerce?

Simply fill out the booking form on our website with your details and preferences. Our team will review your application and contact you within 2 business days to discuss opportunities that match your qualifications and availability.

What are the payment terms for doctors?

Doctors are paid weekly via direct deposit. Home visits earn ₹14,000 per consultation while The Medical Commerce earns ₹8,000 per session. Payments are processed every Monday for the previous week's work.

What equipment do I need for The Medical Commerce consultations?

You'll need a reliable internet connection, a computer or tablet with webcam, and a quiet, private space. We provide the secure The Medical Commerce platform and handle all the technical setup for you.

How does scheduling work?

You have complete control over your schedule. Our app lets you set your available hours, and you can adjust them anytime. You can choose to work day, evening, or overnight shifts as per your preference.

What support do you provide?

We provide 24/7 administrative support, patient scheduling, billing services, and a dedicated account manager. Our team handles all non-clinical aspects so you can focus on patient care.
