Privacy Policy

At The Medical Commerce, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website and use our services. We encourage you to read this policy carefully to understand our practices.

Information Collection: We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you register on our site, subscribe to our newsletter, fill out a form, or engage in other activities. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and any other details necessary to provide our services. Additionally, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing actions through cookies and similar technologies.

Use of Your Information: The information we collect is used to personalize your experience, improve our website, and deliver the services you request. We may use your data to send periodic emails, updates, or promotional materials. Rest assured, we do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only used to enhance our service delivery and communicate important updates.

Data Security: We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Access to your personal data is restricted to authorized personnel who are required to keep the information confidential. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Disclosure: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business, provided they agree to keep your information confidential. We do not permit these third parties to use your personal information for their own marketing purposes.

Changes to This Policy: The Medical Commerce reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of the website after any modifications indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the details provided on our Contact Us page. We are committed to addressing your concerns and ensuring the safety of your personal data as you use our digital services.
